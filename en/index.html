04. FAQ

Common Doubts

How much does a website cost?

Every project is unique. The price depends on the complexity, features, and design. However, I always offer scalable options: from basic solutions to get started to custom developments. Write to me for a free quote!

How long does development take?

A simple landing page can be ready in 1 week. More complex projects, such as e-commerce or custom platforms, can take 3 to 6 weeks. We will define a clear timeline from the start.

Do you work with installment payments?

Yes, I generally work with a 50% deposit to reserve the date and begin, and the remaining 50% upon delivery and final approval of the project.

Does it include support and maintenance?

All my developments include post-delivery bug fixes. Additionally, I offer optional monthly maintenance plans for updates, security, and ongoing support.
